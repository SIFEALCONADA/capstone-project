VoteSphere: PHINMA UI Student Elections Online

This project is a web-based system that facilitates voting online. The students are the ones who interact with the system. Students are provided an online registration account in order to vote. Users are provided with a link wherein they can access it and cast their votes with their specific student leaders.

  • To help the students to engage in voting in elections wherein they can access it everywhere.
  • The online system seeks to address the manual voting system of the school.
  • To design, develop and implement an efficient, user friendly and interactive web-based voting system.

Student online voting system allows every student to have a chance to participate in a student leaderâ€™s election easily. Students save a lot of time making some ballots and also counting paper ballots by hand. This online voting system can be performed simply with your computer or phones. It also emphasizes the manual system that they used in voting. This system enables students and candidates to get fair election results.

Set Up Exellence

Broad Experience

We advise you on voting best practices so that your vote complies with standards for your location, your industry and the specifics of the election or meeting you are running.

Expert Review

You only get one chance to have a great vote the first time, so after you complete setup and testing, an Expert reviews your settings so you are prepared to host a perfect, flawless vote.

Personalized Notice

Reach voters by email, mail, text message, or using your own website, e-newsletter or existing systems with a personalized election announcement for each voter.

Multiple Ways to Vote

Voters can vote remotely or during a meeting using their cellphone, computer, tablet or mail. Voting is completed using a simple, easy to use, tech-friendly ballot

Reports and Graphs
